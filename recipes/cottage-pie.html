Cottage Pie Recipe

Description

Cottage pie is a classic comfort food dish consisting of seasoned ground meat, traditionally beef, cooked with onions, carrots, and sometimes other vegetables, topped with a layer of creamy mashed potatoes, and baked until golden and bubbling. The dish is hearty, flavorful, and satisfying, with the rich meat filling complemented by the creamy and buttery mashed potatoes. Cottage pie is a beloved staple in British cuisine, perfect for warming up on chilly days and providing a comforting meal for the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1¼kg beef mince
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 carrots, chopped
  • 3 celery sticks, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 3 tbsp plain flour
  • 1 tbsp tomato purée
  • large glass of red wine (optional)
  • 850ml beef stock
  • 4 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • a few thyme sprigs
  • 2 bay leaves

For The Mash

  • 1.8kg potatoes, chopped
  • 225ml milk
  • 25g butter
  • 200g strong cheddar, grated
  • freshly grated nutmeg

Steps on how to make cottage pie

  1. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large saucepan and brown 1¼kg beef mince. Set aside.
  2. In the same pan, add 2 tbsp olive oil, onions, carrots, and celery. Cook until soft.
  3. Add garlic, plain flour, and tomato purée. Cook briefly, then return beef to the pan.
  4. Pour in red wine (if using), reduce slightly, then add beef stock, Worcestershire sauce, thyme, and bay leaves.
  5. Simmer uncovered for 45 mins until gravy thickens. Season and remove bay leaves and thyme.
  6. In a separate pot, boil peeled and chopped potatoes until tender.
  7. Drain and mash potatoes with milk, butter, and three-quarters of the grated cheddar. Season with nutmeg, salt, and pepper.
  8. Spoon meat into ovenproof dishes, top with mashed potatoes, and sprinkle with remaining cheese.
  9. If serving immediately, bake at 220C/200C fan/gas 7 for 25-30 mins until golden.
  10. If using a slow cooker, brown mince, then add vegetables, flour, purée, wine, stock, and herbs. Cook on High for 4-5 hours. Make mash, top meat, and bake as above.
