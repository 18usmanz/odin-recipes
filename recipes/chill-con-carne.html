Chilli Con Carne Recipe

Description

Chili con carne is a hearty and flavorful dish originating from Mexico, typically made with minced or ground beef, tomatoes, beans, onions, garlic, and a variety of spices such as chili powder, cumin, and paprika. Known for its bold and spicy flavor, it offers a satisfying balance of heat from the spices, sweetness from the tomatoes, and richness from the beans. Often served with rice or topped with cheese, sour cream, and cilantro, chili con carne is a versatile and comforting meal enjoyed worldwide, especially during colder seasons.

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion
  • 1 red pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 heaped tsp hot chilli powder (or 1 level tbsp if you only have mild)
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 500g lean minced beef
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • ½ tsp dried marjoram
  • 1 tsp sugar (or add a thumbnail-sized piece of dark chocolate along with the beans instead, see tip)
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée
  • 410g can red kidney beans
  • plain boiled long grain rice, to serve
  • soured cream, to serve

Steps on how to make chilli con carne

  1. Chop onion, red pepper, and garlic. Heat oil in a pan, then sauté onion until soft.
  2. Add garlic, red pepper, chili powder, paprika, and cumin. Cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Brown minced beef in the same pan, breaking it into small pieces.
  4. Dissolve beef stock cube in hot water, then add to the pan along with chopped tomatoes, marjoram, sugar, salt, pepper, and tomato purée.
  5. Simmer covered for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Drain and rinse kidney beans, then add to the pan. Simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
  8. Remove from heat, cover, and let stand for 10 minutes to allow flavors to meld.
  9. Serve chili con carne with soured cream.
  10. Enjoy with plain boiled long grain rice.
